Welcome to my Digital Portfolio

This is the landing page for my professional portfolio. Here, I share my work on developing innovative pedagogical strategies for teaching mathematics and science. My goal is to create engaging and effective learning experiences that foster critical thinking and a genuine curiosity for the world around us. Please feel free to explore my teaching philosophy, research interests, and sample projects.

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe in a student-centered approach that leverages technology to visualize complex concepts. The following quote encapsulates my view:

"The art of teaching is the art of assisting discovery." – Mark Van Doren

I strive to create an inclusive classroom where every student feels empowered to ask questions and contribute. You can read more about my specific methods on my teaching page.
